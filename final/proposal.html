Final Project Proposal

built with fisica, Kinect and Max/msp

Basic
This is a project that involves interactions among the motion of the body, vision and music. We plan to use Kinect to track the motion of a body, Processing to draw visual objects and Max/Msp to generate sounds according to the body motion, so that the audience can sort of play with the shapes on the screen and the music using different parts of their body.

Inspirations:
http://www.creativeapplications.net/processing/kinect-physics-tutorial-for-processing/
http://www.creativeapplications.net/processing/geometry-textures-shaders-processing-tutorial/

Goals:
Playful interactions, beautiful vision and pleasing music
